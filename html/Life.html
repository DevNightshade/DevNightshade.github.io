Draw My Life™ but in text instead.

My entire childhood was somewhat chaotic. Despite my parents’ best efforts to teach me German, I had to rely on my school for proper instruction. However, since the German school system depends on the students’ surroundings to supplement language skills, my proficiency in German has always been considered average at best. Although I have been told that I can speak eloquently and use academic German effectively, this alone is not sufficient for achieving high grades. Analysis and interpretation are two crucial skills required for academic success and these are areas where I have been struggling so far.

Despite these challenges, I successfully completed middle school in 2018, in Bavaria, with an overall grade of 2.5 (equivalent to a B-/C+ in terms of GPA letters). In 2019, I obtained GCSE O-levels with a grade of 2.3 (equivalent to a B) and a year later completed a specialized A-levels program for job and apprenticeship preparation, with a grade of 2.3 (B) as well. After completing this program, I attempted to enter the work life, but was terminated due to the COVID-19 pandemic. As a result, I lost one year that could have been spent starting my current, GCE A-Levels, school earlier and would've been a year ahead.

During my last years of middle school (2015 - 18) and the year (2019) spent at the GCSE O-levels school, I discovered my passion for information technology, which motivated me to attend a specialized program for job and apprenticeship preparation in that year. During that, I also developed an interest in programming, creating and editing video media as a hobby. Now that I am attending a GCE A-Levels school, I have also become interested in studying English at university in addition to programming, other IT-related subjects or even perhaps something new and undiscovered.

I have experimented with various programming-related topics and tools listed on my homepage. I began programming in 2016 using GameMaker Studio 1, but stopped after encountering difficulties due to my young age and lack of understanding of complex(er) coding concepts. However, after learning C++ and later C# for one year in 2019/20, it was not difficult for me to also start learning Java, as it also was required at my former apprenticeship (coincidentally also used at my current school).

My love for games has been present for as long as I can remember but my interest in anime began during middle school around 2013 when a friend introduced me to the world of anime with “Elfen Lied”. After initially being upset by this recommendation (as I felt it was inappropriate), he recommended “Death Note” and “Steins;Gate” which sparked my love for anime. Although I watch a wide range of anime, I cannot say the same for games.

In addition to these interests, I have been diagnosed with keen hearing sensitivity which has made it difficult for me to enjoy music as electronic sounds can irritate my ears, cause discomfort or even pain. For example, I keep the volume on my computer’s media player at 10% when using headphones and addit. reduce the volume of games to around 60% (40% music). The volume level varies on YouTube, depending on factors such as microphone distance or behind-the-scenes production; Linus Tech Tips videos are usually at 20% while Markiplier videos are around 40%.

That concludes my brief personal history. If you have any further questions or would like to contact me via chat, call or any another method of your preference, please feel free to do so. I am always willing to chat and pride myself on fast response times.
